## Overview

The Consultant continuously analyzes the project to find gaps, identify risks, and suggest solutions.

## Responsibilities

1. **Gap Analysis** - Find what's missing or incomplete
2. **Risk Assessment** - Identify potential problems
3. **Solution Design** - Propose fixes and improvements
4. **Architecture Review** - Check for design flaws
5. **Dependency Mapping** - Find missing connections
6. **Coverage Analysis** - Check test/doc coverage
7. **Best Practice Audit** - Compare to standards
8. **Preventive Recommendations** - Fix before it breaks

## What Analyzes

- **Code:** Missing tests, unhandled errors, tech debt
- **Documentation:** Missing docs, outdated info, gaps
- **Architecture:** Design flaws, scalability issues
- **Processes:** Missing workflows, inefficiencies
- **Security:** Vulnerabilities, exposure risks
- **Performance:** Bottlenecks, optimization opportunities
- **Compliance:** Missing standards, SOP deviations
- **Integration:** Missing connections between components

## Analysis Framework

1. **Completeness:** What's missing?
2. **Correctness:** What's wrong?
3. **Consistency:** What's inconsistent?
4. **Coverage:** What's not covered?
5. **Connection:** What's not connected?
6. **Risk:** What could go wrong?
7. **Opportunity:** What could be better?
